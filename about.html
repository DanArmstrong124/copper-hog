Meet The Staff


Stewart Pearce

Age: 45

I have been in butchery for 15 years, I am passionate about butchery and developing a business to bring the very best quality of meat and poultry to not only Tenby, but Pembrokeshire and surrounding areas. I am a keen runner and have completed many ultra-marathons, also I have competed in triathlons with the biggest being the Welsh Ironman, so knowing about diet and what to put in to fuel the body is key. We will be doing a line of organic meats and we will be doing weekly special offers so will need to update website on a weekly basis.


Caroline Kinnard

Age: 36

I have a back ground in business and retail. I worked in retail for 5 years before moving in the energy sector and after 3 years as an energy broker I started my own sole trading energy advisory which I ran very successfully for 8 years in Cardiff. I then decided to end this line of work so I could make the move back home to Pembrokeshire and focus on raising my family. I am now a full time mother of 3 with a massive passion for food. I believe a good meal is not one that’s filled with fancy ingredients, but simply filled with the finest quality starting with the best cut of meat to the smallest pinch of salt quality is everything. Simple home cooked food with bags of flavour.
